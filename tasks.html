Challenge Tasks

The OmniIHC2025 Challenge focuses on advancing comprehensive tumor profiling through accurate cell segmentation and quantification in immunohistochemistry (IHC) images. The challenge aims to develop robust computational methods for analyzing multi-stained tissue images across different centers and cancer types.

Cell Segmentation

Task Description
Accurately delineate individual cells within multi-stained tissue images, focusing on precise boundary detection and cell instance separation. This task requires participants to develop algorithms that can effectively identify and segment cells across various IHC staining types.
Evaluation Metrics
  • Panoptic Quality (PQ): Measures the classification accuracy of pixel points, reflecting the correctness of instance segmentation.

Cell Quantification

Task Description
Count cells and measure expression levels of specific biomarkers within the segmented cells, including calculation of Tumor Proportion Score (TPS). This quantification is crucial for understanding tumor heterogeneity and the tumor microenvironment.
Evaluation Metrics
  • Mean Absolute Error (MAE): Computes the absolute error between predicted and true cell counts, as well as the Tumor Proportion Score (TPS).
